I'm a Library & Solution Architect at Rockwell Automation, where I lead the development and release of six device libraries within the Device Object Library (DOL) ecosystem — trusted by industrial customers worldwide.

Beyond automation, I'm passionate about algorithmic trading, building React-based tools for prop firm analysis, Pine Script strategies for XAUUSD, and exploring agentic AI workflows. I believe the best engineers build across domains.

Based in Pune, Maharashtra. Fluent in Marathi, Hindi, and English. Available for senior architecture, technical leadership, and consulting opportunities.
